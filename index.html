What is Save to Google Contacts?

Save to Google Contacts is an chrome extension which allows you to save contact (mobile/email) from webpages directly to your phone for free

Can you tell one use case for this?

If you want to save contact directly from your computer screen to your phone. you can use this extension

Why use OAUTH2

We use OAUTH2 to ask google access to authenticate with firebase
